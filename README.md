# CVSS v5.0 Skull Emoji 💀

**draft-darley-cvss-v5-skull-emoji-01**

*A proposed Internet standard. Published 1 April 2026.*

---

## What is this?

This is a loving, 18-month labour of love: an April Fools RFC in the grand tradition of the IETF's annual humorous publications — RFC 1149 (IP over Avian Carriers), RFC 2324 (HTCPCP), RFC 3514 (The Evil Bit), RFC 5841 (TCP Packet Mood), and their distinguished kin.

CVSS v5.0 replaces the vulnerability scoring system's notoriously complex numerical formulas with a five-skull scale of doom, natively transportable via carrier pigeon and fully compliant with post-quantum encryption (RFC 9999, expires 19 January 2038).

It is affectionate. It is absurd. **It is, we maintain, genuinely useful.**

---

## The backstory

This draft was first submitted to the IETF Independent Submissions Editor in March 2025. The RFC Editor enjoyed it. Publication was deferred to 2026 for production reasons. Still we iterated, adding jokes, and weaving in other April Fool's RFCs as dependencies.

In March 2026, the Independent Submissions Editor (who shall remain unnamed) declined with clarity and grace:

> *"Actually CVSS is the creation of another organization, and I think we should be making fun of ourselves, not others. It's an enjoyable read, tho."*

Fair point, full respect.

**Plan B:** self-publish on 1 April 2026 under Proper Tools, circulate freely, and aim for a substantially revised submission to the IETF for a 1 April 2027 publication — this time even funnier, incorporating self-deprecating commentary on RFC complexity and IETF process rather than on CVSS specifically. If the IETF *just* wants to make fun of itself, we're happy to oblige.

This repository is the home of that collective effort.

---

## The 2027 plan

We are treating this as a proper, if ridiculous, standards development effort.

**The goal:** address the IETF Submission Editor's objection by reframing the document so that the IETF's own processes, traditions, and accumulated RFC archaeology are the proximate target — with CVSS as *the vehicle* rather than the subject.

**How we're working:**
- Monthly editorial working calls (open to all good faith contributors)
- Issues for proposed amendments, new jokes, and structural suggestions
- PRs for proposed text changes
- A LEADERBOARD for the best community contributions

If you want to join a working call, open an issue or email [trey@propertools.be](mailto:trey@propertools.be).
